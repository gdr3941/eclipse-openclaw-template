# Cron Job Templates

Pre-built cron jobs for Eclipse partners. Enable via Telegram by asking your assistant to set them up.

---

## Daily Industry News + Tech Leader Digest

**Schedule:** 7:00 AM local time  
**Purpose:** Morning briefing on Eclipse focus areas + notable statements from key tech/AI leaders

```
Ask your assistant:
"Set up a daily 7am briefing for industry news and tech leader updates"
```

**What it covers:**

Industry News (5-8 stories):
- Semiconductors
- Manufacturing/industrial tech
- Robotics
- Energy (nuclear, solar, batteries)
- Autonomous vehicles
- AI hardware
- Defense tech

Tech & AI Leader Digest:
- **Tech:** Elon Musk, Jensen Huang, Satya Nadella, Sundar Pichai, Mark Zuckerberg, Patrick Collison, Brian Chesky, Palmer Luckey
- **AI:** Sam Altman, Dario Amodei, Demis Hassabis, Ilya Sutskever, Andrej Karpathy, Yann LeCun, Fei-Fei Li, Mustafa Suleyman, Jim Keller

## Inbox Monitor

**Schedule:** Every 30 minutes  
**Purpose:** Alert on important unread emails

```
"Monitor my inbox every 30 minutes and alert me on important emails"
```

## SEC Filing Watcher

## How to Enable

Just ask your assistant in Telegram. Examples:

- "Enable the daily industry briefing at 7am"
- "Set up exec tracking but at 6:30am instead"
- "I want all three morning briefings"
- "Add startup scout but focus on robotics and defense only"

Your assistant will create the cron jobs with your preferences.

---

## Customization

All templates are starting points. You can:
- Change times
- Modify focus areas
- Add/remove companies to track
- Adjust frequency
- Combine briefings

Just tell your assistant what you want.
